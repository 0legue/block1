Nissan Silvia 15

The Nissan Silvia S15 is a sports car that gained popularity
for its sleek design and impressive performance.
Produced by the Japanese automaker Nissan, the Silvia S15 was part of the Silvia series, which has been a staple in the drifting and sports car scene.
Introduced in 1999, the Silvia S15 featured a variety of engine options, including the turbocharged SR20DET engine, which provided ample power for enthusiasts.
Its lightweight construction and balanced chassis made it a favorite among drivers who enjoyed spirited driving and drifting.
The Silvia S15's design incorporated aerodynamic elements that not only enhanced its appearance but also improved its handling at high speeds.
Inside, the car offered a driver-focused cockpit with sporty features and comfortable seating.





Nissan Silvia 13

The Nissan Silvia S13, also known as the 180SX in some markets, is a legendary sports car that captured the hearts of enthusiasts around the world.
Introduced in the late 1980s, the Silvia S13 quickly gained a reputation for its agile handling, sleek design, and affordability.
One of the key features of the Silvia S13 was its lightweight construction and well-balanced chassis, which made it an ideal platform for drifting and performance tuning.
It was powered by various engines over its production years, including the turbocharged CA18DET and SR20DET engines, both of which offered impressive power and torque for its time.




Toyota AE86

The Toyota AE86, also known as the Corolla Levin or Sprinter Trueno,
is an iconic sports car that gained fame for its remarkable performance and legendary status in the world of drifting and motorsports.
Introduced in the mid-1980s, the AE86 quickly earned a reputation for its lightweight construction, balanced chassis, and rear-wheel-drive layout,
making it an ideal platform for spirited driving and drifting. Powered by a 1.6-liter 4A-GE engine, the AE86 offered a perfect blend of power and agility,
delivering an exhilarating driving experience that enthusiasts cherished.




Toyota GR Supra (A90)

The Toyota GR Supra, often simply referred to as the Supra,
is a modern sports car that carries on the legacy of its legendary predecessors while incorporating cutting-edge technology and performance engineering.
Introduced in 2019, the GR Supra marked Toyota's return to the high-performance sports car segment.
Developed in collaboration with BMW, the Supra shares its platform with the BMW Z4, but Toyota has given it its own unique styling, tuning, and character.

Koenigsegg ccx

Volkswagen Caddy

The Volkswagen Caddy is a versatile light commercial vehicle (LCV) produced by the German automaker Volkswagen since 1980.
Over the years, it has evolved from a simple utility vehicle into a modern and practical multipurpose van.
Initially introduced as a pickup truck version of the Volkswagen Golf, the Caddy has expanded its lineup to include various configurations such as panel van, passenger van, and camper van.
This versatility makes it suitable for a wide range of applications, including commercial use, family transportation, and leisure activities.
